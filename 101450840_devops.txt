The COMP 3104 DevOps course is offered as part of the Computer Programming and Analysis program at George Brown College34. It is a 3-hour per week course taught in the 5th semester of the program4. The course covers DevOps principles and practices, focusing on building tools and pipelines for continuous software delivery2. Students learn to create and maintain pipelines, calculate delivery metrics, and evaluate success measures2. Prerequisites for the course include COMP 3044 Linux Essentials4. The course aims to prepare students for modern software development practices in industry
