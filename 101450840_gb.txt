George Brown College is a public institution located in downtown Toronto, Canada, established in 1967. It offers over 170 full-time programs across various fields, including arts, business, health sciences, and technology. The college emphasizes experiential learning, providing students with opportunities to engage in real-world projects and internships. With a diverse student body of approximately 30,000, including a significant percentage of international students, George Brown fosters an inclusive learning environment. The college has been recognized as one of Greater Toronto's Top Employers and is known for its strong industry partnerships. Additionally, it focuses on innovation and research, contributing to community development and economic growth. The campus facilities are modern and strategically located to enhance student experiences. George Brown College continues to evolve with initiatives aimed at equity, diversity, and inclusion in education.
